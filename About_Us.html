ABOUT US
MŌ PARAKORE

Parakore café is a popular, family-owned business that has been operating for 5 years in the Gibbston Valley and has a loyal, local customer base.
The café is owned by Linda Cunningham and her son and daughter-in-law James and Maria Cunningham.
Parakore translates to “pure” in Te Reo Māori and was set-up in conjunction with a large organic fruit and vegetable garden on-site to ensure fresh produce was available seasonally with an emphasis on garden-to-table dining and ensuring a connection to the land.
The Café also sources fresh produce from local suppliers and follows sustainable business practices such as composting and using biodegradable packaging.
